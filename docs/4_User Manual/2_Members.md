# Members

## AxLabs
AxLabs is a blockchain development company specializing in building tools, infrastructure, and applications for the Neo ecosystem. They are known for developing the Neo3 Preview TestNet, the neow3j Java SDK, and contributing to core protocol development. AxLabs also provides consulting and technical support for Neo projects. [Source: AxLabs](https://axlabs.com/)

## Neo Global Development (NGD)
Neo Global Development (NGD) is the primary development and operations arm of the Neo blockchain. NGD is responsible for core protocol development, ecosystem growth, community engagement, and global marketing. They play a central role in driving Neo's strategic direction and supporting developer initiatives. [Source: Neo.org](https://neo.org/)

## COZ
COZ is a global community of open-source developers and designers focused on building tools, infrastructure, and applications for the Neo blockchain. COZ has contributed to key projects such as Neon Wallet, neo-python, and the Neo blockchain explorer. They are recognized for their commitment to open-source collaboration. [Source: COZ.io](https://coz.io/)

## Neo News Today (NNT)
Neo News Today (NNT) is a leading news and media platform dedicated to covering developments in the Neo ecosystem. NNT provides news, interviews, educational resources, and community updates, helping to keep the global Neo community informed and engaged. [Source: Neo News Today](https://neonewstoday.com/)

## Red4Sec
Red4Sec is a cybersecurity firm specializing in blockchain security audits and consulting. They have conducted security assessments for the Neo blockchain and its smart contracts, helping to identify vulnerabilities and improve overall security. [Source: Red4Sec](https://red4sec.com/)

## NeoNEXT
NeoNEXT is a community-driven group focused on research, development, and education within the Neo ecosystem. They contribute to technical documentation, developer tools, and community outreach, supporting the growth of Neo's developer base. [Source: NeoNEXT](https://neonext.io/)

## NeoResearch
NeoResearch is a collaborative group of researchers and developers dedicated to advancing the Neo blockchain through academic research, protocol optimization, and community education. They have contributed to consensus algorithm improvements and technical documentation. [Source: NeoResearch](https://neoresearch.io/)

## NeoSPCC
Neo Saint Petersburg Competence Center (NeoSPCC) is a core development team focused on building infrastructure and tools for the Neo blockchain. They are responsible for projects such as NeoFS (a decentralized storage platform) and NeoGo (a Go implementation of the Neo node). [Source: NeoSPCC](https://nspcc.io/)

## NEOF1
NEOF1 (Neo Force One) is the first OG community group in the Neo ecosystem, founded and led by a long-standing Neo community member. Originating from early Slack groups and later moving to Discord, NEOF1 serves as a central hub for discussion, support, and community-building among Neo enthusiasts, including developers, investors, and key ecosystem figures. The group is recognized for its strong moderation, active engagement, and contributions to connecting and supporting projects and members across the Neo ecosystem. [Source: NEOFORCEONE](https://x.com/NEOFORCEONE)
